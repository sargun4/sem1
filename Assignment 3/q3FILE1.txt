These days, more and more people are making the choice to go to university.
While some people are of the opinion that the only purpose of a university education
is to improve job prospects, others think that society and the individual benefit in much broader ways.
It is certainly true that one of the main aims of university is to secure a better job.

is one of the best ways to do this as it increases a person's marketable skills and
attractiveness to potential employers. In addition, further education is very expensive for
